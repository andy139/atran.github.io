About Me

Hi, I'm Andy Tran. I'm currently a software engineer based in San Francisco, CA.

I love building intuitive and sleek web applications. I'm most excited by creating products that can make an impact and provide a great experience for the user.

Before becoming a software engineer, I was a student at UC Santa Barbara where I studied data science. Shortly after graduating, I joined App Academy's web development program to pursue my dream of becoming a full stack software developer.

When I'm not developing beautiful web applications, I find myself often watching the Ozarks, jamming out to Taylor Swift, or cheering for the Los Angeles Lakers.
